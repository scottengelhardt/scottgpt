Mindstamp is an interactive video platform.
Simply upload your video to Mindstamp, add some interactions and you have created a dynamic, engaging experience for your viewers.
In the videos you can add button, hotspots, images, text, questions and much more.
We have rich analytics allowing you to enhance your videos performance and track your viewers activity.
Mindstamp supports a number of 3rd party integrations including Synthesia, HubSpot, Shopify, Stripe, Salesforce, OpenAI, and many more.
Mindstamp is great for sales, marketing, education, and various other use cases.
